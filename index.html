Revolutinising Film Distribution & Film Industries Across the World
We are video sharing platform focussing on entertainment and aiming to disrupt the exisiting paradigm of film distribution by using a secure digital platform developed using Blockchain Technology, which will provide free to watch shows in a legitimate way, better transparency for advertisers in their engagement with users, enable new ways of incentivising an engaging community (Producers, Users and Advertisers) through the use of tokens which can be en-cashed in fiat money.

Traditional Film Distribution

The Way

Powered by EOS Blockchain
GoViddo products are being developed on EOS blockchain-based technology which provides its own tokens called as Gotokens to its community.

Wider Release & Excellent Recovery Option

After producing a film 95% of the makers find it very difficult to distribute their work through the means of traditional film distribution and to reach to their wider audience resulting huge financial losses. We are providing direct release and recovery options for film makers and creators.
Producers
Any independent film maker, creator, film producer can become a GoViddo Creator and launch a channel which we call as a Production House.

 Creators earn from the advertisement revenue and by GoTokens from users who watch and curate and like the content.

 Can earn upto $200,000 for 1 million views.

Stopping Piracy

Due to online piracy movie producers are losing significant amount of business. With our platform we are creating alternative for piracy by releasing free to watch movies in legit way.
Viewers
View great Exclusive Shows and Movies for free content and get to watch 3 minutes of non-skippable advertisements at the beginning of the content and in the interval (if there is any) of the content.

 GoViddo incentivizes Viewers to engage with the platform by rewarding them with GoTokens for the content they interact with.

 Advertisers directly reward Viewers who choose to watch their adverts.

Opportunity for Advertisers

A legitimate, free-to-watch broadcast platform will unlock high viewer numbers providing a valuable opportunity to advertisers.
Advertisers
GoViddo creates a direct value exchange between brand spend and Viewer interaction, each view will be recorded in a way that is transparently auditable within the GoViddo Blockchain. Ads are non-skippable and advertisers pay to GoViddo in fiat currency enables Advertisers to directly pay Viewers GoTokens in exchange for their attention.

 GoViddo enables Brands and sponsors to transact with Creators directly through the platform using GoTokens

 This assures sponsorships and brand placements are verified through GoViddo Blockchain technology.
